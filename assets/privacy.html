Privacy Policy

How we handle your personal information on haseebzahid.com — clearly, honestly, and without the fine print.

Last Updated: April 2026
Section 01

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Haseeb Zahid ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects the personal information of visitors and students on haseebzahid.com.

By using this website, contacting us through any form, or submitting your details via any lead magnet, you agree to the practices described in this policy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

Our commitment: Your personal information is never sold, rented, or traded with third parties. We use it only to deliver educational value and respond to your inquiries.
Section 02

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide through the following channels on this website:

  • Contact Form — Your name, email address, and WhatsApp number when you reach out with an inquiry.
  • Lead Magnet Popup — Your name, email address, and/or WhatsApp number when you request a free resource (e.g., cheat sheets, market guides).
  • Enrollment Inquiries — Any additional details you share when expressing interest in a course or mentorship package.

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as financial information, national identity numbers, or payment card details directly on this website.

Section 03

How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and answer questions about our courses or services.
  • To send you course information, curriculum details, and enrollment guidance.
  • To deliver free resources (cheat sheets, guides, analyses) that you have requested.
  • To send PSX market updates, analysis, and educational content — only if you have opted in to receive such communications.
  • To follow up on enrollment interest you have expressed on this website.
No spam. We will never send unsolicited promotional messages. Every communication you receive from us will be relevant to the interest you expressed when submitting your details.
Section 04

WhatsApp Communication

By providing your WhatsApp number on this website, you acknowledge that we may contact you via WhatsApp to discuss your enrollment inquiry, share course-related information, or deliver resources you requested.

WhatsApp messages will be sent only in the context of your expressed interest in our services. We will not add you to broadcast groups or mass-message lists without your explicit consent.

Opt-out anytime: If you no longer wish to receive WhatsApp messages from us, simply reply with the word STOP in any conversation. We will immediately honor your request and cease all WhatsApp communication.
Section 05

Data Storage & Security

All personal information you submit through this website is stored securely. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data from unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

  • Your data is never sold to any third party, advertiser, or data broker.
  • Your data is never shared with external organisations except as described in this policy.
  • We retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law.

While we implement best practices to protect your information, no method of data transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to contact us immediately if you believe your information has been compromised.

Section 06

Cookies & Analytics

This website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience and to help us understand how visitors interact with our content. Specifically, we use Google Analytics to collect anonymised data about page views, session duration, traffic sources, and general visitor behaviour.

  • No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected through cookies or Google Analytics.
  • Analytics data is aggregated and used only to improve the quality and relevance of our website content.
  • You may opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

Section 08

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data held by us:

  • Right to Access — You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Deletion — You may request that we delete your personal data at any time. We will comply promptly unless we are required to retain it by law.
  • Right to Correction — You may request that we correct any inaccurate or outdated information we hold about you.
  • Right to Opt-Out — You may opt out of any communications from us at any time, via email or by replying STOP on WhatsApp.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below. We will respond to all data requests within a reasonable timeframe.
